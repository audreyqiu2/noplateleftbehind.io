No Plate Left Behind

A user-centered collaboration with online food delivery services and local restuarants to mitigate food waste.

Learn more

Our Solution

Reduce food waste in landfills

Globally, 3.3 billion tons of greenhouse gases are emitted from food waste in landfills every year.[1]

Distribute and donate unservable food

No Plate Left Behind extends upon existing mobile food delivery services and infrastructure to donate unservable food from restaurants to local food banks.

Protect and preserve our planet

Moving towards sustainable practices protects our well-being.

A User-Centered Design Approach

User-centered design ensures those who benefit from the product have a voice in the outcome.

User group 1: Food delivery service drivers

User group 2: Restaurant workers with food delivery experience

User group 3: Food bank workers

Phase 1: User Research

Research will begin with semi-structured user interviews from all three user groups. The goal of the interviews is to establish a baseline for the user experience of the application in order to gain insight into how to design the No Plate Left Behind extension.

These interviews will uncover pain points and lead to the development of user personas, journey maps, and storyboards to focus on the needs and possible design features for each user group.

Phase 2: Design & Iteration

After gaining better understanding of motivation and goals of users in all groups, design requirements can be determined to start with low-fidelity prototypes. Usability testing will be conducted on these prototypes and changes will be made accordingly. This process will be repeated several times to steadily develop a functioning and satisfactory high-fidelity prototype.

Phase 3: Development

Using high-fidelity and user-tested wireframes, our team will begin develping the No Plate Left Behind extension onto the existing Uber Eats mobile application. This development will include cooperation and communication with mobile software engineers at Uber Eats. The implementation of No Plate Left Behind will be extended to other food delivery service applications as our team iteratively works with each platform's varying interface experience.
